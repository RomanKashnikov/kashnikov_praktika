History and formation

Brand Royal Furniture was founded in 1963, with a production capacity of less than 20,000 parcels and only about 200 invoices. However, an indefatigable, even slightly obsessive pursuit of quality and passion put into work every day has allowed the company to achieve truly excellent results.

Not only did business turnover increase, but the company’s stock of professional experience was also significantly replenished, although the fundamental approach to business remained the same. Royal Furniture continues to develop its philosophy today: beauty, functionality, comfort.
